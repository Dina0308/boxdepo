Who we are

Storage Service Company

Welcome to Boxdepo, your ideal partner for innovative and reliable furniture storage solutions. We offer flexible and secure furniture storage services in Barcelona, ideal for a wide variety of needs, both personal and business.

Our offer ranges from mini-warehouses for personal belongings to large storage facilities for corporate needs. Each space is designed to offer maximum security and accessibility, with customizable storage options to suit your needs.

  • Maximum security for your goods

    The security of your goods is our priority. With state-of-the-art video surveillance systems and strict access controls, we make sure that every box is a safe and secure environment.

  • Flexibility and comfort

    Boxdepo offers 24/7 access to your storage room. This flexibility allows you to access your goods at any time, making our service ideal for the fast pace of everyday life.

  • Customized storage and warehousing solutions

    Every customer has unique needs, and at Boxdepo we work to meet them all. We offer personalized advice to help you choose the space that best suits your needs.
